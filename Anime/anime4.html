Dragon ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a beloved anime and manga series created by Akira Toriyama, centered around the adventures of Goku, a martial artist with incredible strength and a mysterious past. The series picks up where Dragon Ball left off, focusing on Goku's adult life as he works to protect Earth from increasingly formidable threats.

Throughout the series, Goku continually pushes himself to grow stronger, facing a diverse range of enemies, from powerful warriors to deadly androids. Alongside his family and friends, including his son Gohan, the warrior Vegeta, and his loyal companion Krillin, Goku takes part in epic battles that challenge the limits of his strength and determination. As the series unfolds, Goku and his allies encounter powerful new forms of combat, including the legendary Super Saiyan transformation, and face challenges that test not only their physical strength but also their resolve and teamwork
